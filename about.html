Welcome to Elite Fixtures

Building Retail Fixtures Since 2006

Founded in 2006, Elite Fixtures began international expansion in 2012—serving brands across Southeast Asia, South Korea, and Japan. Since 2018, we’ve accelerated our North American presence, with continued growth in both the U.S. and Canada.

Today, we are a trusted manufacturing contractor and long-term partner for commercial renovation firms and large-scale builders—delivering custom retail fixtures, LED display cabinets, shopfitting, and millwork systems with factory-direct quality, reliable lead times, and scalable production.
