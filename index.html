Welcome to Elite Fixtures

We Build Retail Fixtures That Sell

Design • Manufacturing • Shipping • Installation Support. Factory in Huizhou (near Shenzhen), with a Toronto Office—serving brands worldwide.

Beauty / Lifestyle
We create premium fixture systems for beauty and lifestyle brands—wall displays, gondolas, LED showcase cabinets, testers and checkout areas. Designed for high-traffic retail with clean lighting, brand-ready finishes, and flexible merchandising layouts.
Luxury / Collectibles
We design and build high-end display environments for luxury and collectibles—secure vitrines, LED glass showcases, jewelry-style counters, and feature walls. Precision detailing, premium finishes, and clean lighting help elevate product value, while lockable storage and durable construction support daily retail operations.
Food Retail / Pop-up
We deliver fixtures for food retail and pop-up concepts—kiosks, counters, tasting stations, branded carts, and modular shelving. Built for quick setup and easy transport, with practical storage and durable, easy-to-clean surfaces. Ideal for mall activations, seasonal pop-ups, and multi-location rollouts.
